Place the reamining players in your Siege Engines. What classes are placed within them isn't of importance, but what is important is protecting the Demolishers. Make sure whoever is within the Siege Engines is focused on knocking down Pyrite, killing adds, and interrupting Flame Vents.The players being thrown by your Demolisher should have strong single target damage, and the ability to attack from ranged. When you land ontop of Flame Leviathan you cannot swap positions. Incase someone misses the throw or dies, you should have ranged ontop to cover. Because of this Warlocks, Mages & Enhancements are amazing. Rogues & Deathknights also do well.
